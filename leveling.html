Leveling Guide

Leveling in The Realm Online involves gaining experience through combat, quests, and exploration. Focus on building skills with points earned per level (up to 142 at level 200). Start with basic classes like Warrior or Adventurer for easier survivability.

Key Archetypes

  • Warrior: Focus on weapon skills (e.g., Grand Master in a weapon) and Critical Strike.
  • Wizard: Prioritize spell schools like Thaumaturgy and Meditation.
  • Thief: Emphasize dexterity-based skills like Pick Pocket and Acrobatics.
  • Adventurer: Mix melee and magic for versatility.

Leveling Routes and Tips

Begin in low-level areas like Ratling Run (levels 1-6) and progress counterclockwise around Leinster. Use dungeons for partial clears by resetting. For wizards, start with magic-only fights against imps, then move to areas like Imp Haven by level 7.
